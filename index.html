About the Summit

The Financial Future of West Africa Is Being Written in Lagos

Finance West Africa 2026 brings together central bank governors, fintech founders, commercial bank CEOs, regulators, and global investors for a single day of uncompromising dialogue about what it takes to build financial infrastructure at the scale Nigeria demands.

From AI-driven credit rails and embedded finance to CBDC adoption and cross-border payment corridors — this is where strategy meets execution, and ambition meets accountability.

Summit Themes

Three Pillars. One Conversation.

01
🤖

AI & The New Banking Stack

How machine learning, generative AI, and predictive analytics are being deployed across Nigeria's banking system — from fraud detection to hyper-personalised lending.

  • AI Credit Scoring in Low-Data Markets
  • LLMs in Compliance & KYC
  • Agentic Banking: Autonomous Financial Services
  • AI Risk Models for Emerging Markets
02
🔗

Payment Rails & Digital Infrastructure

The architecture underneath the economy — real-time payments, CBDC, open banking APIs, and the cross-border corridors connecting West Africa to the world.

  • eNaira 2.0 & CBDC Adoption
  • PAPSS and Pan-African Settlement
  • Open Banking API Standards
  • Stablecoin Corridors in Trade Finance
03
⚖️

Regulation, Trust & Systemic Risk

Building confidence in a market undergoing rapid transformation — what regulators, banks, and fintechs must do together to ensure a resilient, inclusive financial system.

  • CBN Policy Direction for 2026–2030
  • Fintech Licensing & Regulatory Sandboxes
  • Cybersecurity in Core Banking
  • Financial Inclusion & Agent Banking
